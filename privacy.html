Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 08, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At CT Traders, we collect information that you provide directly to us when you make an enquiry, request a quote, or sign up for our services. This may include:

  • Contact information (Name, Email, Phone Number, Address).
  • Business details (Company Name, Nature of business).
  • Service preferences and enquiry history.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used purely to provide and improve our facility hygiene services. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and process service requests.
  • Communicate regarding maintenance schedules and system updates.
  • Improve our website and product offerings based on user feedback.

3. Data Protection and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your data is stored on secure servers and access is limited to authorized personnel necessary for service delivery.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to ensure our website functions correctly. We do not use intrusive tracking cookies for advertising purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site features may become unavailable.

5. Third-Party Disclosure

CT Traders does not sell or trade your personal information to outside parties. This does not include trusted partners who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, to request corrections, or to ask for the deletion of your data from our active service records.
